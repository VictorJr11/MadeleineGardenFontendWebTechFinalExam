Our Services at Madeleine Garden

Madeleine Garden stands out as the ultimate venue for a wide range of events, whether intimate or grand. Offering services for weddings, birthday parties, baby and bridal showers, graduation celebrations, proposals, conferences, corporate meetings, baptisms, communion, and confirmation ceremonies, the venue provides unmatched flexibility and attention to detail. With its spacious hall accommodating up to 700 guests, scenic garden for outdoor events, luxurious guesthouse, and ample parking, Madeleine Garden ensures that every event is memorable and stress-free.

Who's Madeleine Safari

Born on November 1st, 1980, GITERA SAFARI Madeleine was a highly respected businesswoman known for her intelligence, versatility, and passion. As the founder, creator, and driving force behind Madeleine Garden, she envisioned a space that would bring people together to celebrate life's most meaningful moments.

Madeleine was not only a successful entrepreneur but also a loving mother of six. Her generous spirit extended beyond her immediate family, as she welcomed children from her extended family into her home with open arms. Her home was a haven of love, care, and support, where everyone felt valued and embraced.

Her love for family, friends, and community fueled her work at Madeleine Garden, making it a place where people could come together for weddings, graduations, reunions, and more. Every celebration at Madeleine Garden reflects her meticulous attention to detail and her deep commitment to making others happy.

Outside of her work, Madeleine enjoyed spending time with her family, building strong connections with loved ones, and engaging in community activities. Although she tragically passed away on February 11th, 2024, Madeleine's spirit continues to live on through Madeleine Garden.

Her legacy of love, generosity, and vision remains alive in every event hosted there, inspiring all who visit to celebrate life just as she did—with passion, care, and joy.

Madeleine Safari's Family

Madeleine Safari's family is a Christian family with 8 children, united by love, faith, and dedication. This exceptional family embodies the values of responsibility, passion, and commitment that always characterized Madeleine.

Madeleine's eldest daughter, born on July 11, 2000, was a model of responsibility and dedication. Graduating from Mount Kenya University in 2022, she was deeply involved in realizing her mother's dreams and played a crucial role in creating Madeleine Garden. Tragically, she passed away on February 11, 2024, in the same accident as her beloved mother.

Madeleine's husband, a respected mathematics teacher at Lycée de Kigali for over 20 years, is a pillar of wisdom and strength for the family. Despite the heartbreaking loss of his wife and eldest daughter, he continues to guide his children with love and wisdom while ensuring the continuity of Madeleine Garden.

Victor, Madeleine's son, has taken on the responsibility of Managing Director of Madeleine Garden. Young and passionate, he brings new energy and fresh ideas while preserving his mother's vision. His dedication and hard work are essential to the continued success of Madeleine Garden.

Together, this Christian family of 8 children embodies the spirit of Madeleine Safari: love, generosity, and vision. Despite the challenges, they remain united, perpetuating Madeleine's legacy through Madeleine Garden and inspiring all who meet them with their strength, love, and unity.

Victor – Managing Director of Madeleine Garden

Victor, the son of Madeleine, has taken on the mantle of responsibility as the Managing Director of Madeleine Garden. A young, passionate individual, Victor approaches his role with dedication and enthusiasm, ensuring the continued growth and development of the family's beloved venue. As a student, he has always been committed to learning and bettering himself, qualities that now serve him well as he manages the operations of Madeleine Garden.

Victor oversees every aspect of Madeleine Garden, from event planning to maintaining the beauty of the space. His keen sense of responsibility and love for the venue, which was founded by his mother, drives him to ensure that Madeleine Garden continues to flourish. He brings fresh ideas and youthful energy to the role, making it his mission to preserve his mother's vision while adapting to new opportunities for growth.

As the Managing Director, Victor's leadership is central to Madeleine Garden's success. He manages a team, coordinates events, and ensures that the venue remains a premier destination for celebrations and gatherings. His hardworking nature and genuine love for the garden make him an indispensable part of its future.

Victor comes from a family of six children, including two boys, and has taken on the role of caretaker and leader within the family. His commitment to the legacy of Madeleine Garden reflects his deep connection to his mother's dream and his determination to see it thrive for years to come.
